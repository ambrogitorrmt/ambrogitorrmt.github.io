Here is where we talk about what it's like owning a dog. There are a lot of responsibilites and things to take care of when you own a dog — you can't rush into it!

Owning a dog closely resembles having a child of your own. Domesticated animals depend on humans for many things, and it's our duty to provide not only the things dogs need to survive, but also other facets of animal care such as training, love, and discipline.

Before you jump into getting a dog of your own, here are a few things you should consider:

Can I physically house a dog?
Which breed is best for me?
Can I support both myself and the dog I want to get?
Do my current pets or other people I'm living with get along with dogs?
Does my lifestyle allow me to care for a dog?
